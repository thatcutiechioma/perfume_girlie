Our Story

Power Has a Scent.

Oni was born from a simple but radical belief — that fragrance is not just an accessory. It is an extension of who you are. It speaks before you do. It lingers long after you leave.

Every bottle in the Oni collection is carefully curated — handpicked from the finest fragrances in the world. We don't just sell perfume. We find the scents worthy of the bold, the elegant and the unforgettable.

And this is just the beginning. Oni original signature scents are coming soon — crafted from scratch, built to last and made to make a statement.
